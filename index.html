About

I am a 3rd year Astrophysics DPhil (PhD) student at the University of Oxford. I am interested in the characterisation of exoplanet atmospheres at high spectral resolution, including their atmospheric chemistry, dynamics, and formation. Full details of my research can be found under research (make link). Prior to my PhD I obtained my MPhys (Master's) degree in Physics and Astronomy from Durham University, UK. My full CV can be found here (link CV).
